# PhonePe Transaction Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze transaction performance, customer behavior, service performance, and payment success trends. The dashboard helps businesses monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

## Executive Summary

**Total Transaction Value:** ₹3.47 Billion | **Total Transactions:** 300K | **Total Users:** 108K | **Success Rate:** 96%

The platform processed ₹3.47 Billion through 300K transactions from 108K users while maintaining a strong 96% success rate.

## Key Business Insights

* Loans generated the highest transaction value (**72.89%**), making them the primary revenue driver.

* Bike Loans were the top service type, contributing **24.18%** of total transaction value.

* Mature Users (**35.11%**) and Adult Users (**34.67%**) accounted for nearly **70%** of the user base, while Senior Users (**9.27%**) had the lowest participation.

* July recorded the highest transaction value (**₹304.5M**), while February recorded the lowest (**₹278.9M**).

* Service Error (**33.76%**) and Wrong Information (**33.44%**) were the major causes of transaction failures.

* The Top 5 High-Value Customers contributed a significant share of transaction value, highlighting opportunities for loyalty programs and personalized offers.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts for loan products.

* Introduce loyalty programs and premium benefits for high-value customers.

* Focus marketing campaigns on Adult and Mature users.

* Improve system stability and user validation processes.

* Analyze successful strategies from high-performing months and apply them during low-performing periods.

## Business Conclusion

Loans and Bike Loans are the key growth drivers of the platform. Improving transaction reliability, targeting Adult and Mature users, and rewarding high-value customers can help increase customer satisfaction, retention, and future revenue growth.
